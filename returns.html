Returns & Refunds

Simple returns,
money-back guarantee.

We want you to love what you buy. If something isn't right, we'll make it right — with a straightforward returns process and a money-back guarantee.

①

Request a Return

Contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order ID and the reason for return.

②

Pack & Ship

Pack the item in its original condition and packaging. We'll guide you through the pickup or drop-off.

③

Get Refunded

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund is processed — our money-back guarantee in action.

Return policy

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, in original condition, with all packaging and tags.
  • Product return equals a money-back guarantee, subject to inspection.
  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method after the item is received and checked.
  • Certain hygiene-sensitive items may not be eligible for return once opened.

Damaged or wrong item?

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with a photo. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you.
