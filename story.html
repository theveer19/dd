Years of Craft

Where it started

Dimazo was founded with a simple belief: quality kitchenware shouldn't be a luxury. With more than a decade of hands-on expertise in importing, sourcing, and distribution, our team set out to design durable, functional products built for real Indian kitchens — and priced for real Indian families.

From our manufacturing base in Rajkot, Gujarat, we invested in modern, environmentally-conscious production facilities and automated machinery that holds to the strictest quality norms. Every bottle, lunch box, and chopper that carries our name is made from high-grade, food-safe materials.

What drives us

Our priority has always been the health and well-being of the people who use our products every day. That's why our entire range is BPA-free, leak-tested, and built to last. We're constantly inventing — new designs, new categories, new ways to make cooking and carrying food easier, faster, and safer.
