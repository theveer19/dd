Sustainability

Built to last.
Made to matter.

We believe the most sustainable product is one you don't have to replace. Durability is designed into everything we make — reducing waste one long-lasting product at a time.

♻️

BPA-Free Materials

Every product is made from food-grade, BPA-free materials that are safe for your family and kinder to the environment.

🌱

Eco-Conscious Facility

Our Rajkot manufacturing facility is built to be environmentally friendly, using modern machinery that minimises waste.

🔁

Designed for Reuse

Leak-proof seals and durable builds mean our bottles and boxes replace single-use alternatives for years.

🏭

Make in India

Local manufacturing shortens supply chains, reduces transport emissions, and supports Indian production ecosystems.

📦

Smarter Packaging

We continually work to reduce excess packaging and move toward more recyclable shipping materials.

💧

Reduce Plastic Waste

A single reusable Dimazofly bottle can replace hundreds of disposable ones over its lifetime.

Our commitment

Sustainability isn't a single initiative for us — it's a direction. We're committed to improving the materials we use, the way we manufacture, and the packaging we ship in, year after year. Quality and longevity remain the foundation: a product that lasts is a product that doesn't end up in a landfill.
